Kobe Bryant

The MJ reincarnate.

Kobe Bryant after winning his second NBA championship
  • 1978 - Born on August 23 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Graduates from Lower Merion High School and is selected 13th overall by the Charlotte Hornets in the NBA Draft. Traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997 - Wins the NBA Slam Dunk Contest and becomes the youngest player to ever start an NBA game at 18.
  • 2000-2002 - Wins three consecutive NBA championships with the Lakers alongside Shaquille O'Neal.
  • 2003 - Faces a sexual assault accusation, which is later dropped. The case is settled out of court, and Bryant issues a public apology.
  • 2006 - Scores a career-high 81 points in a game against the Toronto Raptors, the second-highest single-game point total in NBA history.
  • 2008 - Wins the NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) Award and leads the Lakers to the NBA Finals.
  • 2009-2010 - Wins two more NBA championships with the Lakers and is named NBA Finals MVP both years.
  • 2013 - Suffers a torn Achilles tendon, one of several major injuries in the later part of his career.
  • 2016 - Retires from professional basketball after scoring 60 points in his final game.
  • 2018 - Wins an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for "Dear Basketball," based on a poem he wrote.
  • 2020 - Tragically dies on January 26 in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others.
